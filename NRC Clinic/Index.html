WELCOME TO NATURE RESUSCITATE CLINIC

  • NATURE RESUSCITATE CLINICS

    Nature Ressuscitate Clinic was established in 2021. It is based in the rural community of Sibambayani in Bushbuckridge, Mpumalanga
  • Nature Ressuscitate Clinic focuses on supporting Disadvantaged people in the local community of Sibambayani with the aim to improve and provide exellent health Service that the community is lacking.
  • The Clinic will Offer the most recent comprehensive and recognised health care Treatment. It is our hope to restore good health and take responsibility for each resident for them to receive the treatment they need.
  • OUR MISSION and VISION

    Our Vision is to be the Health Consultancy Clinic of Choice in the rural community, a Leader in Quality Services and to ensure effective access to healthcare and safety
  • Our mission is to assess everyone's needs holistically in providing the professional health service and Advice in Sexual and Reproductive Health and Rights(SRHR) which they deserve in making their choices of Health and Sfety as esy and seamless as possible.
